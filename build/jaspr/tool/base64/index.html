Quick Reference

Standard Base64

Uses A-Z, a-z, 0-9, +, / Padded with = to multiple of 4

URL-safe Base64

Uses - instead of + Uses _ instead of / Safe for URLs and filenames

MIME (76-char wrap)

Used in email attachments Line break every 76 characters
